Welcome to the community
for BOSAGORA

The BOSAGORA community operates to create and maintain a healthy ecosystem.
Community channels are open to the purpose and operated and provide participants with a free opportunity to discuss. If you want to check out our news and various opinions, join us now.

Community Guidelines

Our principle is that everyone can freely express opinions and share information.
However, violent and malicious activities in community space can adversely affect the BOSAGORA ecosystem, so it is recommended that these activities be regulated and observed by participants.

Participants who violate these regulations may be excluded from the space at the discretion of the community leader and apply to all spaces (online, offline) authenticated by BOS-Neo. Some community spaces may have additional regulations and participants are responsible for understanding and adhering to the them.

Basic Principle

In the community, you can express your opinions in a considerate and respectful manner.
You can share information and opinions for the benefit of the whole community.

Restricted Behavior

  • Words and actions that slander or attack the other party

  • Words and actions intentionally intimidating the other party.

  • Aggressive language and behavior related to racism, sexism, and religious discrimination.

  • Words and actions that express information and opinions related to sex, drugs, and crime.

  • Disclosure of personal information or information that can infer a specific individual.s

  • Intentional stalking or follower activity.

Compliance and Reporting

BOSAGORA runs a community manager to maintain a healthy environment for the community.
The manager respects the participants in the community and carries out activities to share opinions and information
for the development of the ecosystem. The manager will check, review, and respond to reports of restricted behaviors
that are against community principles.

If you identify any of the restricted actions we have presented in the BOSAGORA community, or if you have been directly or indirectly affected by these actions, please contact community@bosneo.io or contact the manager of the specific community. We will take necessary steps to keep the confidentiality and to protect the rights of the complainant.

As a result of community management, we can give warnings against such restricted behavior of the participants individually and, if necessary, we can take action to eliminate them from the community. The results of these actions
will be publicized in the community to protect the community and the participants’s activities.
